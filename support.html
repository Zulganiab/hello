Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a group?

Simply navigate to the Groups page and click on any group that interests you. You can view all posts and participate in discussions without signing up.

Can I post anonymously?

Yes! Our platform is designed for anonymous participation. You can comment on any post without creating an account.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use this support form to report any content that violates our community guidelines. Include details about the post and we'll review it promptly.

Who manages the groups?

Groups are managed by our admin team. If you'd like to suggest a new group or have feedback, send us a message through this form.
